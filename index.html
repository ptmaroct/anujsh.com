About

I like the part of engineering where product, interface, and systems thinking collide. Not just whether something works — whether it lands, whether it feels intentional, whether somebody would actually want to use it twice.

Over 7+ years, I've gone from shipping frontend-heavy client work to leading teams, building AI systems, and sweating the details on products used by thousands. I enjoy the full stack, but I especially love the last 10% — motion, spacing, microcopy, and the weird little interactions that make software feel alive.

I lead, mentor, and build fast. I care about outcomes, but I also care about craft. The sweet spot is simple: serious systems, playful interfaces.
