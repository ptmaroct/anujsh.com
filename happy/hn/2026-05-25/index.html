Memory has grown to nearly two-thirds of AI chip component costs.

We keep arguing about flops, while the bill is now mostly DRAM.

Epoch AI's new breakdown reframes how to read the AI hardware market. By 2026, HBM and high-density DRAM together account for around 60 to 65 percent of the bill of materials for top-end accelerators. The logic die that everyone obsesses about, even Nvidia's, is now a minority share of the cost.

This explains a lot of strategy in one chart. Why Samsung is paying six-figure bonuses, why SK Hynix has been the market darling, why Micron's stock keeps reacting to AI capex headlines. If you want to understand who actually captures margin in the AI boom, watch the memory supply chain, not the GPU launch keynote.

    Green card seekers must leave the US to apply, Trump administration says.

    A policy shift that ends consular processing inside the country for most adjustment-of-status applicants. In practice it forces hundreds of thousands of people, many on long-pending family or employment cases, to depart and reapply from abroad. The HN thread is mostly engineers and founders with H-1B family members trying to figure out whether their lives just became a chess problem.

    Why is Vivado 2026.1 dropping Linux support for the free tier?

    AMD is quietly relegating the no-cost Vivado tier to Windows only. For FPGA hobbyists and academics, that is a real wrench: open development boards, Linux toolchains, and Vivado's binary blob are how most learners pipeline through to professional work. The thread reads like a slow-motion 'goodbye to a generation of FPGA students'.

    Constraint Decay: the fragility of LLM agents in back-end code generation.

    An empirical paper showing that as you stack realistic backend constraints (RBAC, transactional integrity, schema migrations), state-of-the-art coding agents collapse in quality far faster than benchmarks suggest. The authors propose a constraint-decay metric that correlates better with production failure than pass@k. Useful framing if you are buying or building agentic dev tools.
