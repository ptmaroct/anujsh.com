№ 01 · STORY OF THE DAY · thatprivacyguy.com · ▲ 1.5k pts · 820 comments

Google Chrome silently installs a 4 GB AI model on your device without consent.

“If Gemini Nano is becoming a default Chrome dependency, "it's just a browser" stops being true.”

The story of the day, and deservedly so. Forced on-device model deployment with no opt-in is the kind of thing browsers used to ship competitors over.

Four gigabytes of weights, dropped onto your disk on a normal update — that's a meaningful new category of "installed software," not a feature flag. The interesting fight over the next year isn't whether AI ends up in browsers; it's who gets to consent to it being there.
